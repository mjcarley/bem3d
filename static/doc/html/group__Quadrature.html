Symmetric Gaussian quadrature rules for triangles, taken from Wandzura, S. and Xiao, H., `Symmetric quadrature rules on a triangle', Computers and Mathematics with Applications, 45:1829–1840. If the element e is triangular, the rules are passed unmodified; if the element has more than three corners, the rules are mapped on to subtriangles formed by the element corners and the centroid of the element in intrinsic coordinates.
